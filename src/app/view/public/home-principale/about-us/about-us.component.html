About

Engflexy is the first interactive platform for learning English in the Arab world (100% online). Engflexy's mission is to simplify and facilitate access to English language learning and to build an educational institution that inspires.

Developers

Young people are passionate about achieving their goals to move our region forward by leveraging cutting-edge technology to simplify and improve learning.

Where did the name "EngFlexy" come from?

The name is self-denoting, EngFlexy is a composite word of two words Engo flexy.
Eng-telly for English (English),
Flexy-telly for flexible word (flexible).
The basic idea here is that our platform must be far from the traditional language centers (a building with professors + stacked sections + blackboard), using advanced technology we have developed a flexible interactive platform 100% Online, i.e. the learner chooses the timing and the right place for it in addition there is the possibility of choosing between individual or group classes.
