Learning a language is a long-term commitment and sometimes it feels like we hit a wall and our motivation starts to drop or something unexpected pops up that stops us from studying as frequently as we would like to. Taking a break is totally okay and necessary at times but many students do not have a plan to get back on track which can lead to major language loss that will take months to get back and staying motivated to learn English becomes more difficult. In this Blog,Engflexy shares some advice and strategies to help you identify different types of breaks and how to get motivated and back on track as soon as possible!
